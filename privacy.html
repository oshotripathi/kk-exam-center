Privacy Policy

At KK Exam Center, accessible from https://oshotripathi.github.io/kk-exam-center/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by KK Exam Center and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

KK Exam Center does not require users to create an account. We do not collect personal information such as name, address, phone number, or email address unless a user voluntarily contacts us.

2. Log Files

KK Exam Center follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.
